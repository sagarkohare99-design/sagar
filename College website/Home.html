About the Library

The P.D.N Patil Polytechnic Library supports teaching, learning, and research activities of the institute. The library provides a rich collection of textbooks, reference books, journals, e-resources, and study materials for diploma students and faculty members.

Library Rules

  • Maintain silence in the library.

  • Reference books are for library use only.
