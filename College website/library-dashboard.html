A library is a curated collection of books, media, and digital resources, organized for public or specific group access, serving as a community hub for learning, research, and recreation by offering free access to information, quiet study spaces, internet, and programs, bridging past knowledge with future generations. Libraries preserve culture, foster literacy, and support lifelong learning through physical and virtual materials like books, e-books, films, and databases, managed by trained librarians.
